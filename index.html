Introduction

Are you buying the right fruits and vegtables at the right time? Throughout the year, prices of fruits and vegtables are constantly fluctuating, as some might cost more than others, depending on the season. This website is designed to show you the price fluctuations of all sorts of different fruits and vegtables from the past year. You will also notice how it is sometimes more expensive to buy certain produce during a specific time of the year. For example, pineapples are in season come spring time thus, they are cheaper to buy compared to any other time of the year.

To see more, click on the Vizualizations tab. You will see how much certain fruits and vegtables cost throughout the year. If you think you are spending way too much on produce, this might help you save money on fruits and veggies, while gaining the nutrition you need!
